Hi，你好。我叫徐俊杰，当前核心研究与关注方向为大语言模型（LLM）、多智能体系统（MAS）及智能体记忆（Agent Memory）领域。

教育背景方面，我于 2017-2021 年就读于浙江师范大学计算机科学与技术专业，2021-2024 年在中科大学软件工程专业完成硕士阶段学习。

现任职于 ASML 深圳分公司，主要负责检索增强生成（RAG）、多智能体系统（MAS）相关的 AI 软件研发，以及计算光刻方向的 C++ 软件开发工作。

Hello! My name is Junjie Xu. My current core research and focus areas include Large Language Models (LLM), Multi-Agent Systems (MAS), and Agent Memory.

Regarding my educational background, I studied Computer Science and Technology at Zhejiang Normal University from 2017 to 2021, and then pursued a Master’s degree in Software Engineering at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) from 2021 to 2024.

Currently, I work at ASML Shenzhen, where I am primarily responsible for the research and development of AI software related to Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and Multi-Agent Systems (MAS), as well as C++ software development in the field of Computational Lithography.
